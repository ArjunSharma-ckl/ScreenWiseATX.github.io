Colon Cancer

Educational awareness content based on the ScreenWise flyer.

What it is

Tumor growth of cancer cells in the tissue of the colon.

Who could have it
  • Age 50 and older
  • African ethnicity
  • History of inflammatory bowel disease
  • Family history
  • Obesity
  • Smoking and alcohol use
Risk factors
  • Age
  • Family history
  • Inflammatory bowel disease
  • Obesity
  • Smoking
  • Alcohol
Symptoms
  • Change in bowel habits
  • Blood in stool
  • Abdominal discomfort
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue
Screening, frequency & cost

Colonoscopy every 10 years starting at age 50.

Cost: $1,000–$3,000.

Often covered by insurance.

People’s Community Clinic, Hope Clinic, and CommUnityCare accommodate low-income patients.
