Prostate Cancer

Educational awareness content based on the ScreenWise flyer.

What it is

A tumor that forms in the prostate gland below the bladder.

Who could have it
  • Men over age 65
  • Higher risk in African American men
  • Family history
Risk factors
  • Age
  • Family history
  • Race (African American men)
Symptoms
  • Difficulty urinating
  • Frequent urination at night
  • Blood in urine or semen
  • Painful urination or ejaculation
  • Pain in lower back, hips, or thighs
Screening, frequency & cost

PSA blood test.

Discuss screening starting around age 50.

Cost: $30–$50.

Often covered by insurance.
