Lung Cancer

Educational awareness content based on the ScreenWise flyer.

What it is

Tumor growth of cancer cells on lung tissue.

Who could have it
  • Adults aged 50–80
  • 20 pack-year smoking history
  • Current smokers or quit within last 15 years
Risk factors
  • Smoking and tobacco exposure
  • Secondhand smoke
  • Radon exposure
  • Asbestos and carcinogens
  • Family history
Symptoms
  • Persistent cough
  • Coughing up blood
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain
  • Unexplained weight loss
Screening, frequency & cost

Low-dose CT scan (LDCT) yearly.

Cost: $300–$400.

Covered by Medicare and many insurers.

Volunteer Healthcare Clinic and CommUnityCare accommodate low-income patients.
