Early detection saves lives.

Lung cancer screening with low‑dose CT scans can detect cancer earlier, when treatment is more effective. ScreenWiseATX helps people understand when screening is right for them and where to go next.

Why screening matters

Lung cancer is often diagnosed late because symptoms may not appear early. Screening can identify cancer before it spreads, improving outcomes and expanding treatment options.
