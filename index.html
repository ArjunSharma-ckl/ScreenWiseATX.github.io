WHY IT MATTERS

Cancer screening is a method that not only allows for detection of cancer, but it also is a prevention tactic. Oftentimes, people aren't aware of the frequency at which cancer screenings should be done and miss out on the opportunity of early detection. If we can promote more awareness of early detection cancer, we can catch this disease at an earlier stage, making it more manageable for healthcare professionals to treat. Additionally, screening is often free or covered by health insurance, so taking advantage of this could change your life.

Why Early Detection Matters

Early detection through regular screening can significantly improve treatment outcomes and survival rates. Many cancers, when caught early, are highly treatable and even preventable.
